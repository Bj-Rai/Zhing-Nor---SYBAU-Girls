## About the Game
Zhing Nor is an educational simulation game based on farming. In this game, the player becomes a farmer who grows crops, transports them to town, and sells them in a shop.

The game connects learning with real-life activities like farming, solving problems, and communicating with people.

---

## Theme
Educational Simulation and Farming

---

## Subject Integration
The game includes different subjects:
- Biology (crop growth and farming concepts)  
- Mathematics (solving questions to move forward)  
- Language (communicating with customers)  

---

## Game Concept
The player starts as a farmer and grows crops. After harvesting, the crops are taken to an urban market.

While transporting crops, the player must solve math questions at traffic signals to continue the journey. In the market, the player interacts with customers and must choose the correct responses to complete sales.

The game has different levels, and the final level is more challenging.

---

## Learning Objectives
- Understand basic farming and biology concepts  
- Improve problem-solving skills  
- Develop communication skills  
- Understand how different subjects are used in real life  

---

## How the Game Works
- The player plants and grows crops  
- Crops are transported to the market  
- At traffic signals, the player solves math questions  
- In the shop, the player interacts with customers  
- The game progresses through levels  

At the end:
- The player completes a final challenge  
- A digital certificate is awarded
